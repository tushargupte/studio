Problem

Take an existing mobile app and re-design it's shopping cart with an improved UI.


Design and Prototyping Tools

Sketch 3
Photoshop CC 2017
InVision

Solution

Mobile is a very focused user experience. Users interact with their device while easily being distracted on the go. Hence the experience had to deliver on a singular task flow—which was to add the item to a cart and all associated decisions that go with it—while lightly promoting relevant offers/discounts. The result was a simplification of a complex decision making process in a mobile environment.
